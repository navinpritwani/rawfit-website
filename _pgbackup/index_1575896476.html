About Raw Fit

We offer a holistic approach to build an athlete. Our wide range of group workouts designed and run by well qualified trainers are filled with lot of fun, engagement, sport activities which train your fitness in a most effective and fun way. These group classes are designed for all level of fitness enthusiast above age group of 16 years.

Activities

Boxing

When you are pressed for time in your fitness routine, boxing may offer a solution. Gaining popularity as a workout over the past few decades, boxing can give excellent weight loss results & drastic improvements to overall health.  

Calorie Crush

If you’ve been behind in terms of fitness or exercise or are looking for a tiresome workout, calorie crushing can be one for you. This workout includes high intensity calorie burning exercises, station workout and interval training.

Push & Pull

A strong will to build muscles and gain strength is needed to begin with the push & pull workout. This activity include muscle focussed exercise for your chest, back, legs etc. It completely depends on your body form and physiology. For best results, long term plans are suggested.

Shape & Strength

Don’t let the muscles bluff you. Gain strength and endurance in the bones by shape & strength training. Improved cardiac functions and reduced potential for injury can be your aim doing this workout.

Zumba

Zumba is a physiological as well as psychological exercise which not only helps you to burn calories but also gives you electrifying joy. If you are looking for a full body tone up with a happy drug you can go for this workout. It is actually a combination of more cardio and less strength training.

Yoga

Balance your chakras and boost your physical and mental well-being with Yoga- meaning ‘Union’. It is known as the most comprehensive form of workout, with mindful exercises and meditation. Yogis and yoginis are known for their endurance and flexibility. If you are planning to take care of your body, mind & soul altogether, you might consider Yoga to be the suitable plan.
