Frequently Asked Questions

Why do we need to book class every day?

To provide a sufficient space for workout and personal touch from our athlete we take bookings for all class. We provide a flexible option to cancel your booking anytime before one hour for class timing.

How do we book a class or attend the gym?

Rawfit is fully operational through the mobile application. It’s a hassle-free experience to give you the ease of booking/pausing/transferring your subscriptions as per your convenience. The reward system is an added benefit for all the users. Kindly download the application to enjoy the experience, available for both ios & android.

Are the activities for beginners?

Ofcouse yes, We have programs catering to all age groups and all level of fitness enthusiast . All you need is to step out of your comfort zone and step in to our fitness studio.

What in case I wish to discontinue my subscribtion?

We strongly recommend you to be regular on your fitness journey. In case you change your mind, We offer a unique feature of transfer your subscription.
